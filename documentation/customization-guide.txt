SwiftShip Courier Service - Customization Guide

- Theme colors live in assets/css/style.css under :root variables.
- Dark mode overrides live in assets/css/dark-mode.css.
- RTL layout adjustments live in assets/css/rtl.css and are toggled from the header.
- Public interactions are in assets/js/main.js.
- Dashboard interactions are in assets/js/dashboard.js.
- Tracking search demo parcel IDs are configured in pages/dashboard-track.html.
- Replace placeholder Formspree, Mailchimp, map, and payment links before production use.
- Replace remote WebP images with local optimized assets in assets/images if required.

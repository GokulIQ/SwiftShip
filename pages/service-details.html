Same-Day Courier Service

Priority collection, live tracking, and delivery confirmation for urgent local parcels.

Full description

Built for urgent local drops.

Same-Day Delivery is designed for documents, retail orders, spare parts, and important parcels that need to arrive before the day ends. This static service detail page models a complete booking decision flow before sending the customer to login.

What's included

  • Doorstep pickup in available city and suburban zones.
  • Five-stage tracking timeline in the customer dashboard.
  • Courier notes, vehicle details, and proof-of-delivery placeholder.
  • Optional fragile handling and insurance value capture.

Typical delivery window

2 to 6 hours depending on zone, pickup slot, parcel size, and courier capacity.

Packaging guidelines

  • Use a strong outer box with no loose edges.
  • Add cushioning on every side for fragile contents.
  • Keep labels flat, dry, and visible.
  • Seal liquids in leak-resistant inner bags before boxing.

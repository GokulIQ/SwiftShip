Our story

About SwiftShip

A local courier team built around predictable pickups, careful handling, and transparent tracking.

Mission

Make local delivery feel dependable, not stressful.

SwiftShip exists to give homes, shops, and business teams a courier partner they can plan around: clear pickup windows, honest pricing, and tracking that answers "where is my parcel" before the question is even asked.

Company story

Local delivery with accountable people behind it.

SwiftShip began as a city pickup route for independent shops that needed faster local delivery without losing control of the customer experience. Today the demo service supports households, retailers, offices, and small e-commerce teams with scheduled pickups and clear delivery stages.

Every public page and dashboard page is static for this front-end assignment, but the flows are designed to feel ready for real API integrations.
